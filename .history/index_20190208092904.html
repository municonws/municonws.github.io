About us

Municon West Coast is the successor company to Municon Consultants. Some of our terms and conditions have changed but our equipment, personnel and standard of excellence have not changed. We remain committed to building upon Municon Consultants’ 27 years of service.

Municon West Coast offers the expertise of several California State Licensed Professional Engineers and Geologists with years of practical experience in the construction industry. Our company has provided documentation and instrumentation services on over 1,000 projects in California and Nevada. From the renovations and seismic upgrades of the historic San Francisco City Hall area after the 1989 earthquake to the high-rise residential and office projects changing the City’s skyline today, we have provided our full range of services to both public and private clients.
