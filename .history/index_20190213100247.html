INSTRUMENTATION AND MONITORING SERVICES

Municon West Coast, Inc., is the successor company to Municon Consultants, which was acquired in September 2018. Municon engineers design and install cost-effective instrumentation programs tailored to site-specific project conditions. We provide monitoring data important to the success of your project.


Municon West Coast provides geotechnical and structural instrumentation services for monitoring of heavy construction works including highways, tunnels, deep excavations, heavy fills and bridges.

Our extensive equipment includes piezometers, settlement platforms, tiltmeters, beam sensors and inclinometers. We upload, manage and host all of your content for you on our secure online servers, allowing you to interact with your content and your project team anytime, anywhere.
