Photo AND VIDEO documentation

We began our business photographing 350 destroyed home sites of the Oakland Hills Fire in 1991 and have continued to provide photo and video documentation for public and private projects throughout California and Nevada. High resolution digital photographs and/or video are used to document existing conditions prior to construction, as well as to verify condition after to construction. We also provide construction progress photographs and videos. The photo and video documentation provides the contractor and property owners against unsupported claims of construction-generated damages.



Construction Photographic Survey:

  • Comprehensively includes the construction site and staging areas.
  • Each image is stamped with time, date and location.
  • Serves as a thorough record of the adjacent structures and facilities prior to construction and verifies their condition once construction is completed.
  • Crack gauges installed were required for displacement monitoring
