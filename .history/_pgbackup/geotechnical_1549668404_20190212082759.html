Geotechnical ins

Municon West Coast Remote Monitoring provides geotechnical instrumentation services for heavy construction operations such as deep excavations, highways, bridges, dams, tunnels, and underpinning works. We offer a wide range of geotechnical instrumentation equipment.



  • Inclinometers, traversing (manual), datalogged IPI (In Place Inclinometers) and horizontal inclinometer arrays
  • Settlement platforms and Sondex settlement systems
  • Tiltmeters, both electrolytic and MEMs types
  • Piezometers
  • Strain Gauges on shoring systems
  • Crack gauges for displacement monitoring


Our equipment is fitted with sensors that allow for remote access for regular reporting and/or website data display. We collect data and generate reports on most of our instrumentation remotely, with dataloggers and cellular modems. This allows us to monitor the performance of temporary heavy construction systems in near real time. The combination of state-of-the-art equipment and our remote logging capability has enabled us to provide our clients with economical and high quality service as well as real time monitoring.
